Frequently Asked Questions

When will Professional and Enterprise accounts be available?
We're working hard to launch these features. Pre-order now to get early access and special pricing when they become available!
Who should choose Corporate vs Enterprise?
Professional is for single hotel properties. Corporate is for hotel management companies with 10-100 locations. Enterprise is for large hotel chains with 100+ locations who need advanced enterprise features.
Can I upgrade my account later?
Absolutely! You can upgrade from Basic to Professional, Corporate, or Enterprise at any time. Your data and profile will transfer seamlessly.
What's included in the 7% partnership contribution?
The 7% partnership contribution is only charged when government officials book your hotel through FEDEVENT. We handle all booking management, payment processing, and reservation coordination.
Do you guarantee bookings or leads?
No, we don't guarantee bookings. Our membership plans provide better visibility and advertising to government officials. When they do book through us, we handle everything and take a 7% commission.
Do I need to pay for the Basic account?
No! The Basic account is completely free forever. You only pay the 7% partnership contribution when government officials book your hotel through our platform.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, ACH transfers, and can work with your accounting department for invoice-based billing.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your Professional or Enterprise subscription at any time. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
