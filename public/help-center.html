How FEDEVENT Works

1

Hotel Registration & Approval

Complete your hotel profile with all required information:

  • Hotel details and amenities
  • Meeting spaces and capacities
  • Banking information for payments
  • NET30 payment terms acceptance
  • Purchase Order acceptance

Timeline: 2-5 business days for approval

2

Contract Matching

Our system matches your hotel with government events based on:

  • Location and proximity to event
  • Room capacity and meeting space availability
  • Per diem rate compliance
  • Hotel amenities and services
  • Past performance and ratings

Result: You receive notifications for relevant opportunities

3

Bidding Process

When a matching opportunity arises:

  • Review the event requirements and dates
  • Submit your competitive bid
  • Include room rates, meeting space fees, and terms
  • Highlight your hotel's unique advantages

Timeline: Typically 3-7 days for bid submission

4

Contract Award & Execution

If your bid is successful:

  • Receive official contract award notification
  • Review and sign contract terms
  • Coordinate with government event planners
  • Execute the event with excellence
  • Submit invoices for payment

Payment: NET30 terms with government-backed Purchase Orders

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements to join FEDEVENT?

To join FEDEVENT, your hotel must:

  • Accept NET30 payment terms
  • Accept government Purchase Orders
  • Have indoor facilities (not outdoor properties)
  • Meet minimum room and meeting space requirements
  • Be AAA rated (optional but preferred)
  • Provide proper banking information
How long does the approval process take?

Hotel registration approval typically takes 2-5 business days. We review your profile, verify information, and ensure you meet all requirements before approval.

What payment terms do you offer?

FEDEVENT operates with NET30 payment terms. Government contracts come with Purchase Orders that guarantee payment within 30 days of invoice submission.

How are contracts awarded?

Contracts are awarded based on competitive bidding. Factors include room rates, meeting space availability, location, amenities, and past performance. We encourage competitive but realistic pricing.

Can I update my hotel profile after approval?

Yes, you can update your hotel profile at any time through the dashboard. Changes to rates, amenities, or capacity will be reflected in future contract matching.
